FAQs ON Fat Brain Toys

Question 1: Can I Cancel My Order Before It Ships ?

You can if you are fast enough! Since we process orders quickly (most ship within 24 hours or much, much sooner!), you will need to call our Customer Care staff toll-free at1-800-590-5987, or use Live Chat online, so staff can check the real-time status of your order and let you know if it can be canceled.

Question 2: What Payment Types Do You Accept ?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express credit cards and gift cards, debit cards, Paypal, and Amazon Payments.

Question 3:I'An Amazon Prime Member; Do I Get Free Shipping ?

Amazon Prime member benefits do not apply to orders placed directly through Fat Brain Toys. However, we do offer flat-rate shipping of $3.99 (see shipping FAQ).

Question 4:Do you offer in-store pick-up ?

Yes, we do! Our flagship store is located in Omaha, Nebraska, and our second brick-and-mortar store is located in Overland Park in the Kansas City, Kansas area. If your zip code is near either of our stores, a ship-to-store option will show up on the check-out page. There is no shipping charge to pick up an order in one of our stores, although sales tax is calculated for those locations..
